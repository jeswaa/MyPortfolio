About Me

Hello! I'm Joshua Domingo, and I'm from the little town of Villa Labrador Rizal Nueva Ecija Philippines. I'm a committed student at PHINMA Araullo University , twenty two years old, and I want to be a good front-end developer. My colorful goals and objectives, which I hold dearly, have driven my academic career. I see myself as an expressive person who loves to voice my ideas to people. Another aspect of my personality is engaged, I regularly participate in conversations with individuals and organizations, offering my opinions and learning about different viewpoints. Engaging in talks allows me to gain useful ideas from individuals around me, which helps me to keep learning and expanding my knowledge. I am an active individual who, at heart, connects the layers of life with passion, instinct, and a sincere desire to engage with and improve the world around me.
